Message from the Founder and CEO


The birth of this organization in 2020 was a huge leap of faith and a response to a calling of passion and purpose. Nomatshawe Group, aptly named to honor the memory and legacy of my late mother who is fondly remembered for her warmth, selflessness and love for the development of others, represents the power of women as nurturers, problem solvers, innovators, mentors and most importantly leaders. The cultural flare of the name which originates from the Tshawe clan of the renowned Xhosa Warrior and Leader, Jongumsobomvu Maqoma (Ahh! Jongumsobomvu) aims to be a reminder that our personal and professional growth is best showcased when it is rooted in and celebrates our authentic selves in all that we do not only for ourselves but for the greater good. I therefore invite you to partner and journey with Nomatshawe Group on it's mission to create a world that produces authentic professionals and develops a generation of purpose driven future leaders who will make a much needed impact in business, corporate and government.

Linda Maqoma
